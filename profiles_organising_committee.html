Group Facilitators

The pandemic has plunged us into a new normal, where change is the only constant. Science and its role in the world has also undergone major shifts, as we head into an uncertain future, like sailors sailing towards distant shores.

In the same way these explorers used to navigate uncharted waters by referring to the stars, we see science as a reliable beacon that can help guide us out of these challenging times.

Inspired by this analogy, we chose to adopt constellation names as the names of our ISYFdigital groups. Our hope is that indeed, each group will be inspired by its own constellation name to shine bright and light the way forward.
